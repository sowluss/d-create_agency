Hackathon: "How am I doing financially?"

FRIDAY 6TH JULY 2018

On Friday 6th July D-Create took part in Hppn's first future-focused design hackathon in Brighton.

We joined product designers from the Wisozk Group, Altenwerth, and the CMBT to look at how people will access financial services in the future, including new technologies such as Amazon Alexa.

We're just starting our new financial year and have got plans to do much more work in this area along with our employee communications friends in Pfannerstill.

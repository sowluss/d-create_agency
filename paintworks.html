Paintworks

Creating an online community in Bristol’s creative quarter

Brief

Paintworks is a live, work and play destination in the heart of Bristol’s creative quarter. We were tasked with developing and managing a social media strategy that would raise awareness of the development, connect like-minded individuals, engage potential buyers, and inspire the existing community.

Challenge

By actively monitoring stakeholder conversations across the social media landscape, we were able to gain a unique insight into what would inspire and motivate our target audience to take action. This informed our social strategy, which considered social purpose, engagement tactics, content pillars and customer care policy.

Results

In 2017 we picked up the award for the Best Marketing Campaign: Residential New Build, and the Best Digital Marketing Campaign, for our client Crest Nicholson for building an engaged community in and around Paintworks.

We went on to scoop 'Best use of Digital in the Property Sector' at the Digital Impact Awards and won the 'Best Regional Strategy’ category in The Drum's Social Buzz Awards!

Our client Crest Nicholson commended us on our approach in driving pre qualified leads to this vibrant destination.
